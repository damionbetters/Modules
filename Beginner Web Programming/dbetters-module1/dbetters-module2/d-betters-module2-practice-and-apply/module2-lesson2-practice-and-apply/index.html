What I like to do as an outlet

At times of high stress, I really enjoy activities that soothe my mind. And for me, that just means to go outside for some fresh air. Usually when I am outside, I take a seat under some shade (if it is hot outside). I may enjoy a cigar or a cup of tea. It really depends on what's going on.
